Welcome to Raga Player

Raga Player is an interactive web tool that lets you experience the beauty of musical notes from the comfort of your browser. Whether you're a music enthusiast, a student of Indian classical music, or just curious about ragas, this tool offers a simple way to explore musical notes.

Select a Raga




How to Use:

  1. Enter Notes:
    • In the provided text area, type in the notes you wish to play.
    • Use Western notation (like C, D#, F) or Indian classical notation (like Sa, Re, Ga).
    • Each note should be separated by a space.
    • For a pause, use a comma (`,`). Each comma represents a quarter-second pause.
  2. Play the Composition:
    • Once you've entered the notes, click the Play button.
    • The tool will play each note for 1 second.
    • Pauses (commas) in your sequence will be observed as brief silences.
  3. Experiment and Enjoy:
    • Feel free to experiment with different combinations of notes and rhythms.
    • Discover the unique sounds of various ragas or create your own melodies.

Examples:

  • To play a simple scale: Enter C D E F G A B C'
  • For a Raga sequence with pauses: Enter Sa Re Ga, Ma Pa Dha, Ni Sa'
  • To modify the program to include notation for flat (Komal) and sharp (Tivra) notes, we can use _ to indicate flat notes and # for sharp notes.

Technical Notes:

The website uses the Web Audio API to generate tones, so please ensure your browser supports it for the best experience.

This tool is designed for educational and recreational purposes and is a simplified representation of the rich and complex world of ragas.

Enjoy exploring the world of music with Raga Player!
